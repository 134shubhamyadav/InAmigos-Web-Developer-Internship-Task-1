Who We Are

Get to Know InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation was founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder & CEO, and is headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. It is a Section 8 registered non-profit, licensed by the Central Government, and holds 80G & 12A certifications that ensure transparency and tax-exempt benefits for donors.

The foundation is also CSR-1 registered for corporate partnerships, listed with NITI Aayog in alignment with national development goals, and holds IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality of operations. With a growing network of volunteers and partners, InAmigos now carries its work into 28 states across India.

Our Mission

To serve communities compassionately - directing every contribution toward food distribution, education, women's empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability and social inclusion across India.

Our Vision

A more inclusive, compassionate and empowered society - where every underprivileged person has the opportunity to grow with dignity and respect.

Core Values

  • Compassion

    People and animals in need are met with dignity, not charity as spectacle.

  • Transparency

    80G, 12A, CSR-1 and ISO 9001:2015 status keep our operations accountable.

  • Collective Action

    Volunteers, donors and corporate partners each carry a real thread of the work.

  • Inclusion

    Programs reach children, women, youth, animals and the environment alike.

Our Initiatives

Six Projects, One Ecosystem of Care

Every project targets a specific gap in society - together they form a complete ecosystem of support.

Project Seva

Distributing food and clothing to underprivileged communities - meeting basic needs while fostering dignity and raising awareness about food security.

Beneficiaries: Families facing hunger & inadequate clothing

Impact so far: 50,000+ meals and clothing items distributed

Project Bachpanshala

Bridging educational gaps for underprivileged children through digital literacy training, life skills and school education support.

Beneficiaries: Underprivileged children

Objective: Quality, accessible education for every child

Project Jeev

Empowering lives and spreading compassion through animal welfare - rescue, protection and feeding of animals in need.

Beneficiaries: Stray and vulnerable animals

Objective: Safer, kinder streets for every creature

Project Udaan

Soaring towards a brighter future - women's empowerment through skill development and pathways to financial independence.

Beneficiaries: Women from underserved communities

Objective: Skills that translate into real income

Project Prakriti

"Plant for a better tomorrow" - environmental conservation through plantation drives and sustainability awareness efforts.

Beneficiaries: Local ecosystems & communities

Objective: A greener, more sustainable India

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability among youth through structured skill-development programs geared toward real job outcomes.

Beneficiaries: Unemployed & underemployed youth

Objective: Employable skills, sustainable livelihoods

Why This Matters

From a Single Idea to a Statewide Movement

The Problem

Millions across India still face hunger, limited access to education, restricted economic opportunity for women, animal neglect and environmental strain - often all within the same underserved communities.

Our Response - Founded 2020

Mr. Govind Shukla founded InAmigos Foundation in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh on 23 September 2020, with a vision to build a kinder, more equal society through grassroots, on-the-ground action.

Building Trust

Formal Section 8 registration, 80G & 12A certification, CSR-1 registration, NITI Aayog listing and ISO 9001:2015 accreditation followed - giving donors and partners confidence in how funds are used.

Growing Reach

Six flagship projects - Seva, Bachpanshala, Jeev, Udaan, Prakriti and Vikas - now run in parallel, supported by a volunteer network that has carried our work into 28 states.

What's Next

Deepening partnerships with corporates and government welfare departments, expanding volunteer training, and reaching communities we haven't yet been able to serve.
